For vectors, for the longest time, I always treat vectors like array, without fully understanding it. It seems like I was pretty close. For lists, my assumptions was that they were also like arrays, because I made the connection Python and its List. I learned that I was very incorrect. For deque, my first thought was stacks or a queue, but turns out they are similar to a symmetric nested array.
