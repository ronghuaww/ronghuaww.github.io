What did you think of Exercise #5. Digits.

I felt more prepared for this exercise than the previous one, exercise 4, arrays. After seeing templates so many times—I appreciate Professor Downing reviewing the same concepts over and over again, I felt more comfortable writing constructors and operators. Also, the opportunity to redo our poor attempts was so helpful and a very good learning experience. From our first attempt, we got stuck at implementing to the next item because, in our first design, the iterator only had one item in the list; we only constructed the iterator with the first item. Learning from our first attempt, we realized that in order for the iterator to go to the next object, it needs the whole list, and therefore the whole list can be passed to the iterator constructor.
